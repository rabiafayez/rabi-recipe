Spaghetti with Tomato Sauce

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 2 cups tomato sauce
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil for garnish

Cooking Instructions

  1. Boil water in a large pot, add salt, and cook spaghetti according to package instructions.
  2. In a pan, sauté onion and garlic until translucent.
  3. Add tomato sauce, salt, and pepper; simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. Drain spaghetti and combine with the sauce.
  5. Serve hot, garnished with fresh basil.

Fresh Orange Juice

Ingredients

  • 4 fresh oranges
  • 1 tablespoon sugar (optional)
  • Ice cubes

Cooking Instructions

  1. Peel and segment the oranges.
  2. Blend the segments until smooth.
  3. Strain the juice into a glass, add sugar if desired.
  4. Serve chilled with ice cubes.

Chocolate Cake

Ingredients

  • 1 ¾ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 ¾ cups sugar
  • ¾ cup cocoa powder
  • 1 ½ teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 ½ teaspoons baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • ½ cup vegetable oil
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 cup boiling water

Cooking Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a bowl, mix flour, sugar, cocoa, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  3. Add eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla; mix until smooth.
  4. Gradually stir in boiling water.
  5. Pour into greased pans and bake for 30-35 minutes.
